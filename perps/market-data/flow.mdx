title: "Trade Flow / CVD"
openapi: "GET /v3/perps/flow/{instrument}"
---

Order-flow analytics per time bucket: taker buy vs sell notional, net quantity, and the running **cumulative volume delta** (CVD) — a classic pressure signal. `side` is the taker's direction (long = bought). Choose `bucket` (1m to 1d) and bound with `after` / `before`.

Buckets are **oldest first**, because CVD is a running sum from the start of the window. A plain call therefore starts at the beginning of our archive (July 9, 2026) — for recent flow, pass `after` (e.g. `?after=` 24 hours ago) so the CVD accumulates over exactly the window you care about.
