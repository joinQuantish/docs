---
title: "Username Search"
openapi: "GET /v3/identities/search"
---

Find Polymarket accounts by (partial) username across 5M+ named wallets. Results include `wallet_type`, `deployed`, and `current`, and are ranked by similarity with exact matches first. Not available anywhere in Polymarket's public API.

```bash
curl "https://api.polynode.dev/v3/identities/search?q=swiss&limit=10" \
  -H "x-api-key: $POLYNODE_API_KEY"
```
